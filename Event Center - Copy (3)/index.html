Welcome to Home, Sweet Home, Chicago!

Experience the premier convention destination in North America — McCormick Place, the largest and most versatile convention facility on the continent. Located along Chicago’s stunning lakefront and minutes from downtown, our facility offers over 2.6 million square feet of exhibit space. McCormick Place, consisting of four state-of-the-art buildings, is perfect for conferences, trade shows, corporate events, and exhibitions of any size. With top-tier amenities, cutting-edge infrastructure, and views of Chicago’s skyline, McCormick Place delivers a world-class event experience. Host your next event at McCormick Place, North America’s leading hub for business, innovation, and networking.
